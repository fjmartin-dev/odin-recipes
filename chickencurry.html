INGREDIENTS (serves 4–6)

For the marinade

  • 500 g of chicken (boneless or bone-in)
  • 1 cup of plain yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon of ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon of turmeric
  • Salt to taste

For the curry

  • 2 tablespoons of oil or ghee
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tablespoon of ginger, grated
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder (optional)
  • Fresh coriander leaves (for garnish)

To serve

  • Steamed rice or naan bread
  • Lemon wedges (optional)

STEPS

Marinate the chicken

  1. Mix the chicken with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, turmeric, and salt.
  2. Cover and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes (or overnight).

Cook the base

  1. Heat oil in a large pan over medium heat.
  2. Sauté the onions until golden brown.
  3. Add the garlic and ginger, cook for 1–2 minutes.
  4. Stir in the tomatoes and cook until soft and pulpy.
  5. Add the cumin, coriander, garam masala, and chili powder. Mix well.

Cook the chicken

  1. Add the marinated chicken to the pan.
  2. Cook for 10–15 minutes, stirring occasionally, until chicken is cooked through.
  3. If the curry is too thick, add a splash of water and simmer 5 more minutes.

Garnish and serve

  1. Turn off the heat and let the curry rest for a few minutes.
  2. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves.
  3. Serve hot with rice or naan, and lemon wedges if desired.
