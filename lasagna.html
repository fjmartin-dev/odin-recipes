INGREDIENTES (serves 4)

For the meat sauce

  • 400 g ground meat (beef or mixed)
  • 1 medium onion
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 400 g crushed tomato (or tomato sauce)
  • Salt, pepper, oregano
  • Olive oil

For the béchamel (white sauce)

  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons flour
  • 500 ml milk
  • Nutmeg (optional), salt, pepper

Others

  • Lasagna sheets (pre-cooked or regular, follow package instructions)
  • Grated cheese (mozzarella, Emmental, Parmesan… whatever you have)

STEPS

Prepare the meat sauce

  1. Chop the onion and garlic.
  2. Sauté in a pan with oil until golden.
  3. Add the ground meat and cook until well done.
  4. Add the crushed tomato, salt, pepper, and oregano.
  5. Cook over medium heat for about 15–20 minutes. Set aside.

Make the béchamel (white sauce)

  1. In a saucepan, melt the butter.
  2. Add the flour and mix well (it will form a paste).
  3. Add the milk gradually, stirring constantly with a whisk or spoon.
  4. Cook until it thickens (about 5–7 minutes). Add salt, pepper, and nutmeg to taste.

Assemble the lasagna

  1. In an oven-safe dish:
  2. Cover the bottom with a bit of meat sauce.
  3. Place a layer of lasagna sheets.
  4. On top, more meat sauce, some béchamel, and cheese.
  5. Repeat the layers: sheets, meat, béchamel, cheese… until done.
  6. Finish with a layer of béchamel and lots of cheese on top.

Bake

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (356°F).
  2. Bake for 30–40 minutes, until golden on top.
  3. Let it rest for 5–10 minutes before serving (it improves the texture).
