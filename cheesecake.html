INGREDIENTS (serves 6–8)

For the crust

  • 150 g of Digestive-type cookies
  • 75 g of melted butter

For the filling

  • 600 g of cream cheese (like Philadelphia)
  • 200 ml of heavy cream
  • 150 g of sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon of wheat flour
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract (optional)

For decoration (optional)

  • Red fruit jam
  • Fresh berries (strawberries, blueberries...)

STEPS

Prepare the crust

  1. Crush the cookies until finely ground.
  2. Mix them with the melted butter to form a sandy dough.
  3. Press the mixture into the bottom of a springform pan (20–22 cm).
  4. Chill the crust in the fridge while preparing the filling.

Prepare the filling

  1. In a large bowl, beat the cream cheese with the sugar until smooth.
  2. Add the eggs one by one, mixing slightly after each.
  3. Add the cream, flour, and vanilla, and mix until well combined.
  4. Pour the mixture over the chilled crust.

Bake the cheesecake

  1. Preheat the oven to 170 °C (340 °F) with top and bottom heat, no fan.
  2. Bake for 50–60 minutes, or until the center just barely jiggles.
  3. Turn off the oven and let the cheesecake rest inside for 1 hour with the door ajar.

Cool and decorate

  1. Refrigerate the cheesecake for at least 4 hours, ideally overnight.
  2. Decorate with jam and fresh fruits before serving.
